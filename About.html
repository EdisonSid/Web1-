Edison Ng

(b. 2002, Hong Kong) is a multidisciplinary, primarily working in painting, photography and utilize found objects. His practice explores themes of memory, traces of time, cultural identity and daily observation of surroundings. Putting emphasis on the relationship between the inner mind and the environment.
